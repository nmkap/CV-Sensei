CV Sensei — Logo Concepts

Initial Concepts
Concept 01
The Ascent
Career progression as a visual metaphor. Rising bars represent growth and momentum — the core promise of a stronger CV. Clean, data-inspired, memorable without any character or mascot.
Concept 02 — Selected
The Document Spark
A refined document icon with a folded corner — a universal CV symbol — punctuated by a bold orange spark, suggesting the AI's input that ignites the document. Minimal and instantly recognisable.
Concept 03
The Monogram
A bold, contained mark using the CV initials — the C in brand orange, the V in off-white — set inside a dark tile. App-icon ready and versatile. The wordmark uses a serif/sans pairing for authority with warmth.
Final Refined Logo — Concept 02 Dark Mode
Full lockup

Final Direction

  • Dark background (#222831) adopted as the logo's native context
  • Document outline and corner in off-white (#F2F0EC) for contrast
  • Content lines remain in #6B6560 — subtle, not competing
  • Spark circle stays #E07A15 — the single warm accent point
  • SENSEI wordmark lifts to #E07A15 on dark for legibility and brand consistency
